About Me




Hello! I'm Subhodeep baroi, a passionate and dedicated penetration tester with a strong background in modern computer applications. With a high school education focused on these applications. I've developed a solid foundation in the technical skills required for cybersecurity.

I possess expertise in various penetration testing techniques, including network security, web application security, and exploit development. My skill set extends to tools and technologies like Django, flask, and Python, which I've used in projects like Hackstagram, BugTracker and more.

Currently, I'm focusing on expanding my knowledge in AI and deep learning, exploring how these technologies can enhance cybersecurity practices.

My goal is to contribute to the cybersecurity field by continuously improving my skills, discovering new vulnerabilities, and helping organizations protect their digital assets. I'm excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to collaborating with like-minded professionals to make the internet a safer place.

Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations or opportunities.
